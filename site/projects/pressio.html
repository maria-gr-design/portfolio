Pressio

Pressio is a mobile app for blood pressure tracking, designed for older adults who find existing health apps overwhelming. Using AI tools for research and design, I'm vibe-coding the product step-by-step through iterative prompting. The focus is accessibility, clarity, and ease of sharing data with family and doctors.
