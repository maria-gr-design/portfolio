Designer
& Illustrator

Based in Greece, working remotely with brands and teams around the globe.

My work sits at the intersection of brand, illustration, and digital. I move between art direction, visual systems, and product thinking. I like problems that require both craft and curiosity. Extra points if solving them makes a difference in someone's day or life.

I've always been curious to explore new ways to create: from the most analog, such as embroidery and clay, to the most tech-forward, like AI. Research, detecting patterns, and understanding behaviour are as much a part of who I am as the making, and I'll take any opportunity to work in those areas.

When I'm not at my desk, I'm usually hand-crafting, reading, or spending time in nature around animals.
