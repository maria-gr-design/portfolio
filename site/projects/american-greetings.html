American Greetings

American Greetings is a greeting card and digital messaging platform. Working with the Digital Design Director, I designed a badge system for their membership product to encourage engagement and repeat usage. The system spans milestones, seasonal achievements, feature exploration, and mobile challenges, all integrated within the platform.

Role Art Direction, Illustration
Tools Illustrator, Procreate
Year 2025

Challenge

The badge system needed to scale across 30 achievements with room to grow, while remaining consistent. Each badge had to work across multiple UI backgrounds without losing clarity. The visuals also needed to feel distinct across categories while staying aligned with the brand.

Approach

I explored a few visual directions and selected one that balanced clarity, playfulness, and scalability. A shared badge shape and composition rules kept all designs connected while allowing variation across concepts. Each badge was developed by translating its achievement into a clear visual idea, with future animation considered during design.

Outcome

Delivered a complete set of 30 badges for the initial release, covering multiple categories and use cases within the platform.
