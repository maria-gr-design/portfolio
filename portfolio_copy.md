## Project Descriptions

### DESIR

**DESIR**
Fictional fragrance brand. Bottle concept, creative direction, campaign.
Generated using Adobe Firefly.

---

**Why this project**
I wanted to test whether AI tools could deliver campaign-ready assets with a consistent brand language — not experiments, but work that could actually run.

**The concept**
The object came first. A heart-shaped perfume bottle wrapped in faux fur, with a silver chain connecting the cap to a hoop on the body, like a piercing.

**The direction**
The bottle had a specific tension: soft and restrained, feminine and firm. That tension needed a visual world that matched it. Dark, minimal, realistic. Not romantic, not floral.

All elements were directed through prompting: hardware details (chain, hoop, engraved cap), casting (mixed-race, late 20s, minimal styling, silver piercings, black clothing), references (avant-garde, editorial).

**The process**
Early outputs felt generic — romantic lighting, soft focus, floral tones. I iterated until the visual language matched the concept. The result is a consistent brand style that scales across formats.

### Farm Sanctuary

**Farm Sanctuary, Humane Education**

Illustration and graphic design for a K-12 curriculum. Long-term collaboration with Farm Sanctuary.

**The brief**
Educational materials that made complex and sensitive topics approachable for students, from elementary to high school.

**The work**
Illustrations, infographics, activity books, teacher documents, and presentation materials. Visualizing complex phenomena and concepts with simple, friendly graphics. Memorable depictions that engage children through storytelling.

**The approach**
Warm and inviting visuals. Simplicity for quick production. Consistency across formats so the program feels like one voice. The visuals had to invite curiosity about topics most people look away from.

**The impact**

### American Greetings

**American Greetings**

Gamification badge system for a membership platform.

**The brief**
American Greetings was developing a badge system to keep members engaged. They explored visual directions with several designers and selected my proposal to develop further.

**The direction**
I developed a visual world that is playful, consistent, and scalable. Each badge needed to feel like part of the same universe while rewarding specific user actions.

**The process**
I received badge names and reward triggers, then proposed concepts, starting from sketches evolving through iterations and feedback. The visual style inspired the team to pursue micro-animations when badges are earned to add another level of joy and engagement.

**The work**
30 badges delivered, with more planned. From sketch to final.

*Role: Art Direction, Concept, Illustration*
*Client: American Greetings*

### Ruby Labs

**Ruby Labs, Able App**

Illustration system for app onboarding. Senior Product Illustrator role.

**The context**
Able was a lifestyle app focused on nutrition and wellness. The team needed illustrations for first-time user onboarding and goal setup, with plans to expand into educational content (daily stories, tips).

**The direction**
Flat, playful, colorful, friendly. Simple enough for fast production and easy for others to reproduce. The goal was to build an illustration design system that could scale.

**The work**
New illustration style for onboarding. In-app screens, story templates, and foundations for a broader system. A/B tested against existing styles, rated higher for warmth, relatability and early engagement.

**The team**
Coordinated two junior illustrators. Presented work directly to stakeholders. Part of a cross-functional product team working in Agile.

## Portfolio Design Specification

### Design Direction Summary

**Vibe:** Clean but alive. Structured but not rigid. Confident without shouting. Current without chasing trends. Typography that says "I design" without screaming it.

**References explored:** Basement Studio, Studio Feixen — movement, current feel, bold but controlled.

---

### Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Background | Off-white | #F8F8F8 |
| Text primary | Off-black | #141414 |
| Text secondary | Gray | #555 |
| Text tertiary | Light gray | #888 or #999 |
| Accent | Deep indigo | #4338CA |
| Borders | Light gray | #E0E0E0 |
| Dark section bg | Near black | #0A0A0A |

---

### Typography

**Body/Intro:** System font (system-ui, -apple-system, sans-serif), regular weight (400), clean and readable.

**Project titles:** 36-38px, weight 600, slight negative letter-spacing (-0.02em)

**Section labels:** 11-12px, uppercase, letter-spacing 0.15em, weight 600, color #999

**Name in header:** Explore avant-garde/wavy fonts for distinctive feel:
- Whyte Inktrap (has ink traps, "cut" feeling)
- Migra (wavy, distorted)
- GT Alpina (unusual curves)
- Or custom CSS letter manipulation for wavy effect

**Intro statement:** Large (clamp 28px-44px), regular weight (400), max-width ~720px
