I'm based in Greece, working remotely with brands and teams around the globe.

My work sits at the intersection of brand, illustration, and digital. I move between art direction, visual systems, and product work. I like problems that require both thinking and making.

I've always been curious to explore new ways to create: from the most analog, like embroidery, to the most tech-forward, like AI. A big part of me likes research, analysis, and finding patterns, so whenever I get the chance I step into those areas too.

I find inspiration in art, nature, crafting, and books. I love animals, walks & a good talk.
